In our basketball camp, we will teach your kids the fundamental skills of basketball, including but not limited to dribbling, shooting, and defense. Our coaches are well seasoned and are even on the school team, further showing their qualification to teach the sport of basketball. Your kid will learn a lot and have fun in the process!

Our math courses are flexible and teach based on your child's skill level, so that all children are engaged in learning. Our tutors are all in advanced math courses, maintaining A grades. They have experience teaching their peers the subject, so you can be assured that your child will be taught in a way that they can understand!

Computer science is a difficult topic for younger kids to wrap their head aorund, and due to that, we have prepared a cirriculum that allows for kids with no experience all the way to most experienced coders to learn and be engaged. All of our tutors are highly experienced and have been coding for most of their lives; they will be able to teach your kid what they need to know in order to succeed in High School Computer Science and beyond.
